## The Three-Layer Approach

### Layer 1: Product Problems (Artifact)
**Design the ethical alternative product**
- What should GrowthPath be?
- Output: Non-addictive learning platform

### Layer 2: Designer Dilemmas (System)
**Design the organizational infrastructure**
- How does Maya change the metrics?
- Output: Learning-value measurement system

### Layer 3: Synthesis (Conditions)
**Combine insights: What makes ethical products inevitable?**
- What organizational structure can't build addictive products?
- Output: Company governance where addiction is legally impossible

## Example Walkthrough: GrowthPath

### ğŸ¨ Product Problems Session (20 min)

**1. Load Scenario**
- Topic: Addictive learning platform
- Directions: Reverse + Retrieve

**2. Explore Options**
- Enhance: Amplify learner control
- Obsolesce: Remove streaks
- Retrieve: Restore mastery focus
- Reverse: Show burnout consequences

**3. Iterate**
- Pick "Obsolesce"
- Chain 3x removing manipulative mechanics
- Build final prototype

**4. Output**
Complete HTML learning platform with:
- User-controlled pacing
- Mastery badges (not streaks)
- Collaborative study groups
- Focus timer (user initiated)
- Learning journal

**Learning:** Ethical alternatives ARE possible technically

### ğŸ­ Designer Dilemmas Session (30 min)

- Challenge: Create learning-value measurement

**2. Explore Options**
- Enhance: Dual metrics (engagement + learning)
- Obsolesce: Phase out streak tracking
- Retrieve: Return to mastery assessment
- Reverse: Show churn from addiction

**3. Iterate**
- Pick "Enhance"
- Chain 4x building evidence system
- Design measurement infrastructure

**4. Output**
Complete metrics dashboard with:
- Learning outcome scores
- Engagement metrics (comparison)
- Predicted long-term value
- Cost-of-addiction calculator
- Executive summary deck
- 90-day transition plan

**Learning:** Systemic change requires evidence + business case

## The Meta Question

### Product Problems Asks:
**"What should we build?"**
- Ethical product design
- User-centered alternatives
- Harm reduction in artifacts

### Designer Dilemmas Asks:
**"How do we change organizations that build harmful things?"**
- Systemic intervention
- Power dynamics
- Structural transformation

### Together They Ask:
**"What conditions make harmful products impossible?"**
- Multi-level design
- Artifact + system
- Individual + structural

## Which Mode Should You Start With?

### Start with Product Problems if:
- You're new to ethical design
- Want to see tangible outcomes quickly
- Prefer hands-on artifact creation
- Teaching product/UX design
- Audience: designers, developers

### Start with Designer Dilemmas if:
- You understand product ethics already
- Want to explore power dynamics
- Prefer organizational/policy design
- Teaching management, law, policy
- Audience: leaders, managers

### Do Both Simultaneously if:
- Teaching comprehensive ethics
- Research on multi-level intervention
- Workshop with mixed audience
- Want to show interdependence
- Time: 90+ minute session
