Technical Specifications

Format: HTML/CSS animation, 2.35:1 aspect ratio simulation

Duration: ~8 seconds (universal TILTH opening) + ~6 seconds (episode-specific world) = ~14 seconds total

Typography:

  • Headlines: Oswald Condensed (fallback for Trade Gothic Condensed)
  • Tech accents: Orbitron (fallback for Eurostile Extended)
  • System text: Courier (monospace)

Film aesthetic:

  • 16mm grain overlay (animated noise texture)
  • Chromatic aberration (cyan/magenta plate shift)
  • Newsprint halftone in shadows (simulated via gradients)
  • Subtle gate weave (grain animation)

Audio cues: Visual indicators shown at bottom-left. In production, replace with actual audio stems.

Shared opening sequence (0:00–0:08): Identical across all 8 episodes—mycelium crackle, seed splitting, keyboard-to-soil transformation, feed icons becoming roots, TILTH logo reveal. This creates series continuity.
