WORK‑SENSE — Fictional Company Dossierpublic brief

WORK‑SENSE is a cooperative media‑systems studio that builds low‑tech, high‑reliability tools for ethical work: investigation decks, scenario editors, and time‑aware interfaces. The company’s thesis: ethics is a system you can operate.

Founded: 2025 Type: Worker‑owned co‑op HQ: Sim Francisco Motto: "Clarity, then courage."
Mission • Strategy • Advantagecompany core
Mission
Make ethical practice operable in everyday work.
Strategy

Seven Fields Mapcompany language

AFFECTIVE • WEATHER

How it feels to work here now. We monitor vibe, mood, energy; we DO something if it drifts.

INTERPERSONAL • TERRAIN

Presence, warmth, resonance, friction. Teams are paired by chemistry, not just charts.

CULTURAL • SOIL

Ethos: "Ship truth." Practices: write post‑narratives; reward risk‑taking with receipts.

SIGNALING • AIR

Signals, frames, metaphors. We argue with rules; we show our math.

STRUCTURAL • FRAME

Rhythm, clarity, momentum. Weekly cadence, decision records, reversible vs. one‑way doors.

COGNITIVE • VIEW

Mindset: investigative. Awareness over certainty. Intuition logged, then tested.

TEMPORAL • RIVER

Tempo, cadence, season. Time is a first‑class design material.

Operational Flow • One Day

01 — Check the Weather

Pulse Meter flags a wobble in mood; PM schedules a 10‑minute reset.

02 — Pair by Chemistry

Resonance map suggests a swap; friction drops.

03 — Frame the Work

Switch to “Experiment” frame; risk posture loosens, ideas increase.

04 — Make Time the Interface

Break‑out from Month→Week in Time Alley; reveal hot spots.

05 — Record the Argument

Hypothesis Log captures a decision and its falsifier.

06 — Post‑Narrative

We tell what the rules argued; update Ethics Index with a fresh example.
