Professional Identity Field Guide

Eight archetypes of ethical practice under constraint

About This Field Guide

Based on research by Chivukula, Watkins, Gray & Agerfalk (CHI '21), we identified 8 professional identity archetypes that emerge when tech workers face ethical dilemmas under structural constraint.

These are not personality types. They are positions you take when the system forces a choice between doing what's right and protecting your career, visa status, team relationships, or economic security.

In the Feed Loop investigation, you'll encounter scenarios where Agronica workers embody these identities. You'll choose which position you would take. Then you'll see what actually happened—and why individual ethics can't fix infrastructural violence.
