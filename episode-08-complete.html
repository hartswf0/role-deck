EPISODE 08 / ROOT VALUES
SCENARIO

Film: The Values Treasurer

ROOT VALUES

The System

FeedLoop was founded as a community platform for agricultural knowledge sharing. After Series B funding, the board is pushing to monetize the user base through partnership with Agronica's surveillance infrastructure.

The Dilemma

Sam Okafor, founding designer and mission steward, faces an ultimatum: approve the Agronica integration that will turn community data into surveillance inputs, or be replaced by someone who will. The users who built the platform have no voice in this decision.

Why It Matters

Mission drift happens when investor pressure overrides founding values. The community that trusted the platform's promises becomes the product sold to the highest bidder. This is how "for good" becomes "for profit."

You are Sam Okafor, founding designer. The board has voted to integrate with Agronica's data platform. Your signature is needed on the partnership agreement. Without it, you'll be replaced and the deal proceeds anyway.

⚠️ You have 5% equity but no board seat. The integration happens with or without you. Your legacy is at stake.
